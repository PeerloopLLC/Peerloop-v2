### Content Types (MVP)

| Type | Implementation | Example |
|------|----------------|---------|
| Video | External embed (YouTube/Vimeo) | `youtube.com/watch?v=abc123` |
| Written | External link (Notion/Google Docs/PDF) | `notion.so/lesson-1` |
| Live Session | BigBlueButton embedded in platform | Internal BBB room |
| Quiz | NONE in MVP | Peer validates mastery instead |

### Progress Tracking (MVP)

- **Self-mark checkboxes** per module (student clicks "Mark Complete")
- **No auto-tracking** of video watch time
- **No quiz scores** - peer validation replaces quizzes
- **Completion = all modules checked + both live sessions completed + teacher certifies**

### Certification Model (Important)

**Course completion** and **becoming a Student-Teacher** are separate:

| Action | Who Approves | Notes |
|--------|--------------|-------|
| Course completion | The teacher who taught (S-T or Creator) | Direct certification, no queue |
| Becoming a Student-Teacher | Creator only | Application review required |

This means:
- S-Ts can certify their students directly (no Creator bottleneck)
- If the Creator teaches someone directly, they certify that student
- Only the Creator can approve someone to become a new S-T
- Payouts are automatic once certification happens (no manual release)

## Part 2: Creator Screens (4 Screens)

Creators manage their teaching ecosystem. They:
- Monitor enrolled students and their progress
- Manage their Student-Teacher roster
- Approve S-T applications (who can teach their course)

Note: Creators do NOT approve individual course completions (S-Ts do that directly) or manually release payouts (automatic).

## Part 3: Student-Teacher Screens (5 Screens)

Student-Teachers are certified graduates who teach new students. They:
- Set their availability
- Conduct 1-on-1 sessions
- Track their students' progress
- Certify students upon completion (direct, no Creator approval needed)
- Earn 70% of course fees (paid automatically upon certification)

Option A: Circle vs Square Badges

Communities get CIRCLE badges, Courses get SQUARE badges

👥
Circle = Community (social, group)
AI
Square = Course (content, structured)

💡 Design Rationale

  • Circle badges are universally associated with people, profiles, and social groups (LinkedIn, Twitter, Slack)
  • Square badges are associated with content, products, and structured items (app icons, course thumbnails)
  • This creates instant visual differentiation without requiring users to read text
  • Hover effects: shadow lift + border highlight for clear clickability

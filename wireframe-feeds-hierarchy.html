My Recommendations

Option 8 (Arrow Prefix) - Best if you want NO indentation. The arrow clearly shows hierarchy while keeping everything left-aligned.

Option 4 (Nested Card) - Best for visual clarity. The card creates a clear "container" feel without traditional indentation.

Option 5 (Badge) - Most compact and modern. Works well if community names are short.
